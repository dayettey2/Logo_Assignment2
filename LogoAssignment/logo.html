Colored Logo

The colored logo features two women of color in a reflective view holding hands. The reason why I chose to design the logo that way is to evoke a sense of unity, support, community, and resourcefulness as women are going through pregnancy during and after birth. This Logo Is also features a single-lined circle and can be used on both print and digital materials. The colored version of the logo uses a color scheme that pairs well against a lighter or darker background.

Black and White Logo

The black and white logo can be used in instances where color is not needed or preferred on both print and digital materials.

Reverse Logo

The reverse logo should be used against black or darker backgrounds.
